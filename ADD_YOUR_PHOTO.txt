================================
HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTO - SIMPLE STEPS
================================

STEP 1: Save Your Photo
------------------------
- Save your photo as "profile.jpg" in the portfolio folder (D:\portfolio\)
- Photo should be square (500x500 pixels is best)
- File size should be under 500KB


STEP 2: Update index.html
--------------------------
1. Open index.html file
2. Find line 69-71 (search for "hero-image")
3. Replace this code:

    <div class="image-placeholder profile-photo">
        <i class="fas fa-user-circle"></i>
    </div>

   With this:

    <div class="image-placeholder profile-photo">
        <img src="profile.jpg" alt="Uttam Kumar">
    </div>

4. Save the file
5. Refresh your browser


ALTERNATIVE: Use Online Image
------------------------------
If you want to use an online image:

1. Upload your photo to: https://imgur.com/upload
2. Right-click on uploaded image â†’ Copy Image Address
3. In index.html, use:

    <img src="PASTE_IMAGE_URL_HERE" alt="Uttam Kumar">


That's it! Your photo will appear in a circular frame with animation.
